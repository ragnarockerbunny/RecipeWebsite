Toasted Cheese Recipe

Description

Melted cheese between two slices of toasted bread

Ingredients

  • Slice of Cheddar Cheese
  • Two slices of bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter both slices of bread
  2. In pan or sandwich toasting machine lay bread butter side down
  3. Put cheese on bread
  4. Close sandwich with other slice of bread
  5. Close toasting machine or out lid on pan
  6. If pan frying, flip after two minutes
  7. Remove from heat after four minutes
